👨‍💻 Introduction to Cyber Security

Cyber security protects computers, networks, and personal data from cyber threats. 🔍🛡️

Why does it matter? To keep our personal information and identity safe! 🔑

💡 Image: hacker with a lock. Background: light. Animation: soft fade.

🔍 Why is Cyber Security Important?

  • 🔹 Protects personal data from hackers.
  • 🔹 Prevents phishing & scams. 🎭
  • 🔹 Ensures social media safety. 📱
  • 🔹 Guards against viruses & malware. 🦠

💡 Infographic of cyber threats. Background: dark mode. Slide-in effect.

⚠️ Common Cyber Threats

  • 🦠 Viruses & Malware: Harmful software.
  • 📧 Phishing: Fake emails steal data.
  • 💻 Hacking: Unauthorized access.
  • 💰 Ransomware: Hackers demand money.

💡 Show phishing email example. Background: warning sign. ⚠️

🛡️ Safe Internet Practices

  • 🔐 Use strong passwords (Aa1!#).
  • 🚫 Don't click unknown links.
  • 📲 Update antivirus software.
  • 📶 Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking.

💡 Image: password strength meter. Background: simple white. ✅

📱 Social Media Safety

  • 🚫 Don't accept unknown friend requests.
  • 🔒 Enable 2FA for security.
  • 📢 Report fake profiles & bullying.

💡 Secure social media profile image. Background: blurred chat. 📩

🎮 Online Gaming Safety

  • ✔️ Use trusted gaming platforms.
  • 🗣️ Be careful in chat rooms.
  • 🔑 Secure gaming accounts.
  • 🚩 Report suspicious activity.

💡 Show game security settings. Background: game-themed. 🎮

🚨 How to Stay Safe from Cyberbullying?

  • 🚫 Block bullies, don't respond.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Tell parents or teachers.
  • 📷 Take screenshots as proof.
  • 🔐 Set strong privacy settings.
